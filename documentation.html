What Is an APA?

An Agricultural Protection Area (APA) empowers landowners to secure their property against unauthorized zoning changes for up to 20 years.

Why Should You Use an APA?

APAs provide long-term security for landowners, protecting valuable agricultural land from urban development pressures.

How Does the APA Process Work?

The APA process includes gathering required documents, submitting an application, and reviewing local ordinances.

Role of Our Services

We simplify the APA application process by managing documentation, offering expert consultations, and ensuring successful submissions.
